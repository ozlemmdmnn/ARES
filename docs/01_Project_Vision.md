# ARES Project Vision

## Project Name

ARES (Artificial Response & Emergency System)

---

## 1. Project Purpose

ARES is an AI-assisted disaster management platform designed to help citizens and emergency response teams before, during, and after disasters.

The system aims to support decision-making by analyzing risks, managing resources, and providing intelligent recommendations.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

During disasters, information is often scattered, response times are delayed, and resources are not used efficiently.

ARES aims to solve these problems by creating a centralized command and decision support system.

---

## 3. Mission

To develop a reliable, intelligent, and scalable disaster management platform that can save time, resources, and lives.

---

## 4. Vision

To become one of the most advanced open-source disaster management systems, integrating artificial intelligence, mapping technologies, and real-time emergency management.

---

## 5. Target Users

- Citizens
- AFAD
- Municipalities
- Hospitals
- Search & Rescue Teams
- Emergency Coordinators
